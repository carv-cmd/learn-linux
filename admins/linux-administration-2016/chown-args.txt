
- chown -
-----------|----------|
 ARGUMENT  |  RESULTS |
-----------|--------------------------------------------------------
   bob     | Changes ownership from its current owner to user 'bob'
-----------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 bob:users | Changes ownership from current owner to user 'bob' & file group owner to group 'users'
-----------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 :admins   | Changes a group owner to the group 'admins
-----------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------
   bob:    | Changes from current owner to 'bob' & owner of login group to group bob
-----------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------
